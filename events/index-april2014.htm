Responding to the contained space found within two empty glass library cases on the staircase in the Great Hall, independent curator Lisa A. Banner (IFA, Ph.D. 2006) has come up with a series of intimate exhibitions of conceptual work by contemporary artists. Read more

The Institute of Fine Arts offers a diverse array of public programs throughout the academic year. Programs include annual lecture series, special lectures, symposia, and conferences which cover an extensive range of time periods and genres.

All public programs take place at the Institute, at 1 East 78th Street, unless otherwise noted.

Public lectures at the Institute of Fine Arts are made possible by our generous supporters. Please make a gift today to help the IFA continue providing superior public programming for years to come.
