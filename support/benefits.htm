The Connoisseurs Circle: Benefits of Membership

  Friend $7,500
  • Auditing privileges for select IFA courses
• Invitations to exclusive art world events throughout the academic year:
– Exhibition tours led by curators and faculty
– Visits to studios of prominent artists
– Viewings of New York’s finest private art collections
• Invitations to annual IFA events
– Fellowship Donor Luncheon
– Holiday Party
• Preferred seating at IFA public programs
• Access to IFA research libraries
• Recognition, at the Friend level, in all public donor listings
   
  Patron $10,000
  All benefits of the Friend membership, plus:
• Invitation to IFA Graduation ceremony and reception
• Invitations to VIP exhibition opening receptions at NYU’s Grey Art Gallery
• A special Institute of Fine Arts memento
• Invitation to art day trips outside New York City
• Recognition, at the Patron level, in all public donor listings
   
  Benefactor $15,000
  All benefits of the Patron membership, plus:
• Opportunity to visit the Institute’s archaeological excavations in Egypt, Sicily,
Turkey and Greece
• Invitation to an intimate dinner with IFA Director Patricia Rubin, select faculty and students
• Opportunity to spend time with a faculty member at a cultural institution abroad
• Recognition, at the Benefactor level, in all public donor listings
