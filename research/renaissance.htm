The New York Renaissance Consortium

Welcome to the newly established New York Renaissance Consortium, whose purpose it is to bring together scholars, students, curators, and others with interest in the arts of the Renaissance.  The Consortium is a network for information on research and activities in the New York area. It will hold a number of Consortium events each season, such as panels and discussions.

You can also stay current with events, lectures, conferences, symposia, and exhibitions in the area through this website and by joining the Consortium listserv through the link below: http://lists.nyu.edu/read/all_forums/subscribe?name=ifa-nyc-ren-consortium

Please let us know if you would like to be a part of the Consortium and participate in its growth and development by contacting us directly at ifa.nyc.ren.consortium@nyu.edu.
