Egbert Haverkamp-Begemann


John Langeloth Loeb Professor Emeritus in the History of Art; Adjunct Professor of Fine Arts; Coordinating Scholar, Robert Lehman Collection Scholarly Catalogue

Ph.D. 1958 University of Utrecht, The Netherlands

Research interests

Dutch and Flemish art; history of prints and drawings.

Sample courses

Studies in Rembrandt
The Art of Drawing in The Netherlands
Studies in Rubens

Selected Publicationas

Haverkamp-Begemann, Egbert, Hercules Seghers, and Eduard Trautscholdt.Hercules Segers: the complete etchings. Amsterdam: Scheltema & Holkema, 1973.

Corpus Rubenianum Ludwig Burchard. Vol. X. The Achilles Series. Brussels: Arcade Press, 1975.

Rembrandt: the Nightwatch. Princeton: Princeton University Press, 1982.

The Robert Lehman Collection. Vol. VII. Fifteenth to Eighteenth-Century European Drawings. New York: Metropolitan Museum of Art; Princeton: Princeton University Press, 1999.

Selected Honors

Member, Royal Academy of Sciences, The Netherlands;
Officer, Order of Orange-Nassau, The Netherlands;
Coordinator, Scholarly Catalogue, The Robert Lehman Collections;
Guggenheim Fellowship, 1995-1996
Honorary Fellow, Pierpont Morgan Library, 1995
