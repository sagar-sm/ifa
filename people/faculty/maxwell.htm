Robert A. Maxwell


Associate Professor in the History of Western European Medieval Art


PhD History of Art, Yale University
MA, MPhil History of Art, Yale University
AB, Medieval Studies, Princeton University

Research Interests

Sculpture, architecture, illuminated manuscripts, particularly of the Romanesque and Early Gothic periods; medieval and contemporary theories of the image; approaches to narrative imagery; post-structuralism and word-image relationships; inscriptions in/on/of art; secular illumination; notions of artistic value, discernment, enchantment and forgery.
