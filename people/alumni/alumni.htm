Alumni

Since 1932 the Institute has conferred more than 4,000 degrees. We are tremendously proud of our graduates whom have gone on to be leading historians, archaeologists, conservators, art administrators, curators and writers.

Institute alumni enjoy an array of receptions, cultural outings and scholarly programs that are designed just for them. Groups such as the Alumni Association and the Recent Alumni Circle make it fun and easy to support and engage with the Institute. Life at the Institute continues far beyond graduation.
