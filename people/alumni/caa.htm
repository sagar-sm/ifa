IFA @ The College Art Association

The 2015 CAA conference will be held in New York City. Please find a list of IFA's faculty members, students and alumni who presented at the 2014 conference below. If you are an IFA student, faculty member or alum presenting at the 2015 CAA conference and would like to be included on this webpage, please email us the details at publicaffairs.ifa@nyu.edu.
