IFA Alumni Association

Institute alumni who make an annual gift of $75 or more receive membership to the Alumni Association. They receive the following benefits, for one year, upon making their gift:

•Personalized Alumni Association membership card
•Access to the James B. Duke House
•Use of the Stephen Chan Library
•Invitations to the Fall and Spring Alumni Receptions
•Subscription to the Alumni Association’s Alumni Newsletter

Join your fellow alumni in supporting your alma mater. Your gift is an investment in your own degree.
