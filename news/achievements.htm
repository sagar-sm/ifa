Alumni Achievements

The Institute of Fine Arts has a robust community of over 2,000 alumni, living in 43 different countries worldwide. Institute alumni hold positions in major museums, auction houses, galleries, publishing houses and universities in New York City and beyond. We are proud to share some recent appointments and awards earned by our alumni.
